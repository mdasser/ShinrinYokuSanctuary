Our Story

A Sanctuary Born From the Forest Itself

Inspired by the Japanese practice of Shinrin-Yoku — forest bathing — this retreat was created as a place where nature does the healing. In the quiet Hilltowns near the Berkshires, SYS offers something rare: unhurried time, deep stillness, and the restorative presence of ancient woodland.

Whether you come for a night, a weekend, or a guided experience, you leave feeling something you didn't expect — whole.
